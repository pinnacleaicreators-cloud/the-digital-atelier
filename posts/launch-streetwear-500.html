title: Launch a Streetwear Brand with $500

date: 2026-05-21

---

The barrier to entry for fashion used to be $20,000. You needed inventory, a photoshoot, and a website.

Today, you need $500 and a laptop.

Step 1: Design with AI (Cost: $0)

Forget hiring a graphic designer on Upwork for $300. Use generative design tools to create your graphics.

* Prompt: "Cyberpunk typographic logo, vector style, white on black, distress texture."

* Tool: Imperium Studio (or Midjourney v7).

Step 2: The "Ghost" Photoshoot (Cost: $0)

This is where most brands die. They buy samples, hire a photographer, and rent a studio.

Instead, use AI Product Photography.

Take your flat design file, map it onto a generated model in a generated location ("Tokyo Alleyway, Neon Rain").

The customer cannot tell the difference. The vibe is identical. The cost is zero.

Step 3: The Pre-Order (Cost: Shopify Monthly Fee)

Do not print the shirts yet.

Launch the store with your AI photos. Run TikTok ads with your AI video assets.

Collect the money *first*.

Step 4: Manufacturing (Cost: The rest of your $500)

Once you sell 50 units, *then* you print them. You use the profit from the sales to pay the manufacturer.

This is the Zero-Inventory Model. It is the only safe way to build a brand in 2026.
