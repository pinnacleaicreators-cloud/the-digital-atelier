title: Pre-Order is King

date: 2026-05-21

---

Inventory is cancer.

It eats your cash. It takes up space. And if it doesn't sell, it kills your company.

The Old Way: Guess & Pray

1. Guess what people want.

2. Order 1,000 units.

3. Pray they buy it.

4. Discount the leftovers by 70%.

The New Way: Demand-Driven Production

With AI visualization tools like Imperium, you can show a product that doesn't exist yet.

You can create a "Virtual Drop."

The Protocol:

1. Tease: Post the AI-generated campaign on Instagram.

2. Drop: Open a 48-hour pre-order window.

3. Manufacture: You sell 342 units? You make 342 units.

4. Ship: 4 weeks later.

"But customers hate waiting!"

False. Customers hate *generic* products.

They will wait 4 weeks for something unique, limited, and high-quality.

Telfar proved it. Supreme proved it.

Scarcity is a feature.

Use AI to build the hype. Use Pre-Order to protect the bank account.
